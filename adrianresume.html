My Leadership Experience:

Through Boy Scouts I have learned many leadership skills, and have taken the National Youth Leadership course so that I can use my skills to benefit my community.

The EDGE Method:

  1. Educate. The first step is to verbally educate the person you are trying to teach
  2. Demonstrate. The next step is to physically demonstrate your skill to help the other person visualize what to do.
  3. Guide. The next step is to guide the other person through the skill. Through trial and effort, they will eventually learn the skill.
  4. Enable. The final step is to enable the person. By doing this, they have now learned the skill, and are able to fluently perform it.
